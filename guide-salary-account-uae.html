Best salary account in UAE: how to compare the real fit

A salary account should make payday, rent, bills and daily spending simple. The best option is the one that keeps fees low for your actual monthly pattern.

MOVO Guides · Updated 23 June 2026

Payday
Plan first

Compare these items

  1. Minimum salary or salary-transfer requirement.
  2. Monthly fee and fall-below-balance fee.
  3. Debit card, ATM and transfer costs.
  4. Linked savings options and app features.

Check the salary rule

Some accounts advertise benefits only when salary is transferred to the bank. If you change jobs, become self-employed or receive income irregularly, the account may behave differently. Read the eligibility conditions before you apply.

Calculate monthly cost

Do not compare accounts by one fee. Compare the monthly bundle: account fee, balance rule, ATM habits, transfer needs and card usage. A "free" account can become expensive if it does not match your salary cycle.

Build a payday flow

After salary arrives, separate fixed bills, spending money and savings. A bank with pots, easy transfers or clear notifications can reduce mistakes even if another bank has a slightly shinier offer.

Use the MOVO payday guide to build the routine, then compare banks on MOVO bank ratings.

Salary account checklist

  1. Will salary meet the account rule every month?
  2. What fee appears if it does not?
  3. Can you pay rent and bills easily?
  4. Can you move savings out of sight quickly?
