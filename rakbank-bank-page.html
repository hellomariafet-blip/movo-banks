What people like
Good mobile app Fast opening Fast scanning No minimum balance Multi-currency

Customers consistently praise Wio for its incredibly fast account opening process — most report being fully set up in under 5 minutes. The mobile app is frequently cited as one of the best in the UAE, with a clean interface and reliable performance. The savings rate and lack of minimum balance are particularly valued by expats.

What people don't like
No physical branches Slow ID scanning Chat-only support

The absence of physical branches is the most common complaint — users dealing with complex issues (e.g. frozen accounts, visa changes) find the chat-only support insufficient. Some users report that the Emirates ID scanning step during onboarding requires multiple attempts.
