Best money transfer from UAE: compare the total cost

The cheapest transfer is not always the one with the lowest fee. Compare how much the recipient gets after fees, exchange rate and delivery method.

MOVO Guides · Updated 23 June 2026

Recipient gets
More matters

Compare these items

  1. Exchange rate and visible fee.
  2. Delivery speed and payout method.
  3. Transfer limits and required documents.
  4. Total amount the recipient receives.

Look at recipient amount

A provider can show a low fee and still be expensive if the exchange rate is worse. Always compare the final recipient amount for the same send amount, currency and payout method.

Decide how fast it needs to arrive

Instant or same-day transfers can cost more. If the transfer is not urgent, a slower option may be better value. If it is for rent, school fees or family support, reliability can matter more than saving a few dirhams.

Compare the exact route

UAE to India, UAE to Philippines, UAE to UK and UAE to Europe can behave differently. Compare the exact corridor before each important transfer.

Use MOVO transfers to compare options, then confirm the final price inside the provider before sending.

Transfer checklist

  1. Use the same send amount for every provider.
  2. Compare recipient amount, not just fee.
  3. Check delivery method and timing.
  4. Save proof of transfer until it arrives.
